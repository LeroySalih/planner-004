This is the Section 1 layout design. A few things to note:

  • The amber "Review" button on each lesson row links directly to the marking/feedback page for that lesson
  • The green "Live" dot in the top bar shows the SSE connection is active — counts update in real time
  • The "Assignments →" link in the top bar lets the teacher navigate to the full assignment manager
  • Lessons with no submissions yet are shown greyed out at the bottom of the queue
  • Mentions require a new pupil_note field on submissions (or a new comments table)
