What happens when you click a class card?

Each card shows class name + pupil counts in the 3 score bands. When the teacher clicks one…

A

Navigate to unit-progress-reports

Go to /unit-progress-reports/[groupId] — the existing pupil × unit matrix for that class. Reuse what's already built.

B

Expand inline

The card expands on the dashboard itself to show the per-pupil breakdown without navigating away.

C

No click action

Cards are just summary widgets. Teacher uses the sidebar nav to go to detailed reports separately.
