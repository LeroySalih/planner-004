What does "progress" mean to you?

This will determine what numbers and visuals appear on the dashboard

A

Average score trend

Show how the class average score (0–100%) has changed over the selected time period. e.g. "Class average went from 62% → 71% this week"

B

Per-pupil score deltas

Show each pupil's score change: who improved, who declined, who stayed flat. A list or table sorted by biggest improvement.

C

Both: class summary + pupil breakdown

A headline showing the class trend, with a drill-down into individual pupil changes beneath it.
